Criteria description

  • 1. ICU total occupancy percentage

    It is the total occupancy percentage of ICU (Intensive Care Unit).

    0% - 20%
    20% - 40%
    40% - 60%
    60% - 80%
    80% - 100%

  • 2. Expected ICU total occupancy percentage 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected total occupancy percentage of ICU, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    0% - 20%
    20% - 40%
    40% - 60%
    60% - 80%
    80% - 100%

  • 3. ICU COVID-19 occupancy percentage

    It is the occupancy percentage of ICU, but only of COVID-19 patients.
    0% - 15%
    15% - 30%
    30% - 45%
    45% - 60%
    >60%

  • 4. Expected ICU COVID-19 occupancy percentage 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected COVID-19 patients occupancy percentage of ICU, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    0% - 15%
    15% - 30%
    30% - 45%
    45% - 60%
    >60%

  • 5. Ratio ICU patients with COVID-19 / total ICU patients

    It is the percentage of COVID-19 patients in ICU by the total amount of ICU patients.
    0% - 20%
    20% - 40%
    40% - 60%
    60% - 80%
    80% - 100%

  • 6. Hospitalized total occupancy percentage

    It is the total percentage of patients hospitalized, which also means the number of occupied beds in the hospital.
    0% - 20%
    20% - 40%
    40% - 60%
    60% - 80%
    80% - 100%

  • 7. Expected hospitalized total occupancy percentage 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected total percentage of hospitalized patients expected, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    0% - 20%
    20% - 40%
    40% - 60%
    60% - 80%
    80% - 100%

  • 8. Hospitalized COVID-19 occupancy percentage

    It is the percentage of hospitalized patients, but only of COVID-19 patients.
    0% - 15%
    15% - 30%
    30% - 45%
    45% - 60%
    >60%

  • 9. Expected hospitalized COVID-19 occupancy percentage 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected COVID-19 percentage of hospitalized patients expected, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    0% - 15%
    15% - 30%
    30% - 45%
    45% - 60%
    >60%

  • 10. Ratio hospitalized patients with COVID-19 / total hospitalized patients

    It is the percentage of COVID-19 hospitalized patients by the total amount of hospitalized patients
    0% - 20%
    20% - 40%
    40% - 60%
    60% - 80%
    80% - 100%

  • 11. Amount of COVID-19 positives/100.000

    It is the number of COVID-19 positives per each 100.000 inhabitants since the start of the pandemic
    0 - 5000
    5000 - 10.000
    10.000 - 15.000
    15.000 - 20.000
    >20.000

  • 12. Expected amount of COVID-19 patients 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected number of COVID-19 positives per each 100.000 inhabitants since the start of the pandemic, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    0 - 5000
    5000 - 10.000
    10.000 - 15.000
    15.000 - 20.000
    >20.000

  • 13. Amount of recovered COVID-19 patients/100.000

    It is the number of recovered patients of COVID-19 per 100.000 inhabitants since the start of the pandemic.
    95.000 - 100.000
    90.000 - 95.000
    85.000 - 90.000
    80.000 - 85.000
    <80.000

  • 14. Expected recovered COVID-19 patients 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected number of recovered patients of COVID-19 per 100.000 inhabitants since the start of the pandemic, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    95.000 - 100.000
    90.000 - 95.000
    85.000 - 90.000
    80.000 - 85.000
    <80.000

  • 15. Ratio of recovered COVID-19 patients / COVID-19 patients

    It is the percentage of recovered patients of COVID-19 by the number of positives since the start of the pandemic.
    95% - 100%
    90% - 95%
    85% - 90%
    80% - 85%
    <80%

  • 16. Amount of dead COVID-19 patients/100.000

    It is the number of dead patients because of COVID-19 per 100.000 inhabitants since the start of the pandemic.
    100 - 500
    500 - 1.000
    1.000 - 1.500
    1.500 - 2000
    >2.000

  • 17. Expected dead COVID-19 patients 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the expected number of dead patients because of COVID-19 per 100.000 inhabitants since the start of the pandemic, calculated with the predictive mathematical model.
    100 - 500
    500 - 1.000
    1.000 - 1.500
    1.500 - 2000
    >2.000

  • 18. Ratio of dead COVID-19 patients / recovered COVID-19 patients

    It is the percentage of dead patients because of COVID-19 by the number of recovered patients of COVID-19 since the start of the pandemic.
    1% - 2%
    2% - 3%
    3% - 4%
    4% - 5%
    >5%

  • 19. Average age

    It is the average age of the population with residence in an area.
    <40
    40 - 50
    50 - 60
    60 - 70
    >70

  • 20. Average age of sick patients

    It is the average age of people who have been confirmed of COVID.
    <40
    40 - 50
    50 - 60
    60 - 70
    >70

  • 21. Actual medical personnel / 100 patients

    It is the actual amount of personnel per 100 patients.
    80 - 100
    60 - 80
    40 - 60
    20 - 40
    0 - 20

  • 22. Expected medical personnel needed 1, 3 and 7 days later

    It is the amount of personal per 100 patients that will be necessary to assure the quality of treatments.
    0% - 2.5%
    2.5% - 5%
    5% - 7.5%
    7.5% - 10%
    >10%

  • 23. Reserve of medical supplies

    It is the amount of equipment that the hospitals in an area have stored.
    80% - 100%
    60% - 80%
    40% - 60%
    20% - 40%
    0% - 20%

  • 24. Daily material consumption

    It is the average material consumption in the last 15 days
    0% - 5%
    5% - 10%
    10% - 15%
    15% - 20%
    >20%

  • 25. Lockdown level

    It is the level of restrictions set in the area of people assigned to the hospital. If the level is high, the supply will be reduced and the control measurements inside the hospital will also have to be more strict.
    0 - 2
    2 - 4
    4 - 6
    6 - 8
    8 - 10

  • 26. Transport system level

    It is the level of the transport system that delivers supplies to the hospital. It is taken into account the time of delivery, distance that travels, number of vehicles, etc.
    0 - 2
    2 - 4
    4 - 6
    6 - 8
    8 - 10

  • 27. Weather type

    It is the weather in the area assigned to the hospital.
    0 - 2
    2 - 4
    4 - 6
    6 - 8
    8 - 10

  • 28. Vital companies / 1.000 hospitalized patients

    It is the amount of companies for each 100 patients, which supplies are vital for the proper operation of the hospital, with materials such as water, food, first need materials, etc.

    8 - 10
    6 - 8
    4 - 6
    2 - 4
    0 - 2
